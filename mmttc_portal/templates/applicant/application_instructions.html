INSTRUCTIONS TO FILL APPLICATION FORM ONLINE

*** Courses will be conducted in online/offline mode. ***

Step 1: To initiate filling application online, click the "Apply Online" button below. Fill the details and submit the form. After submission, kindly take a print out of the application form by clicking "Print Application Form" button.

Please note that a unique Application Number is generated for your form. This application number along with your Date of Birth will be required for restarting the session again, uploading the scanned copy of duly signed form, or printing your application form. Please note down and keep the application number safe.

Step 2: The form printed in Step 1 should be duly signed by you and forwarded by the Head of your Institution.

Step 3: Visit this page again through the main website and upload the scanned copy of the duly signed application form by clicking "Upload Signed PDF File" button.

Step 4: After a few weeks, an automated email may be sent to you if your form is "Provisionally Accepted". You may also visit this portal again to check whether your application is accepted or not by clicking "Check Your Form Status".

Note

  • Acceptance of your online application is provisional and subject to verification of facts.
  • According to new guidelines of UGC-MMTTC, participants attending programmes in residential mode will bear the expenses of TA and DA.
  • Participants may also have to pay for accommodation and dinner, if availed.
  • MMTTC, BHU will provide breakfast and working lunch only.
  • No registration fee has to be paid.
